In the town of Gitenburg, life was easy for the people. Crime and other conflicts was at an all time low and bad harvests were only stories used to scare the children. Everything was great except for a young boy. The citizens made his life a living hell.
His biggest dreams were to be a hero. He always wanted to defeat the evil wizard who's been terrorizing the city for decades. But little does they know that the evil wizard  will consume them all.
